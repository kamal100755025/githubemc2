About Us


Welcome to Pisces World, where we make fishkeeping a unique and stress-free hobby! Our mission is to nurture your passion for aquariums by offering healthy, vibrant fish raised on natural, nutrient-rich diets like daphnia, moina, and brine shrimp. For optimal health, we supplement their diet with high-quality nutritional food, ensuring every fish you purchase thrives.


We take pride in crafting an engaging experience for hobbyists by introducing cutting-edge features. From customized aquarium toys created with advanced 3D printing technology to unique accessories tailored to your tank’s needs, we aim to redefine fishkeeping.


Our specialized plants are perfect for low-CO2, low-light environments, making them ideal for beginners and experienced enthusiasts alike. Along with live food to keep your fish active and happy, we provide expert advice to enhance your aquascaping journey.


At Pisces World, we’re more than just a store. We’re a partner in creating serene, beautiful habitats. Let’s keep fishkeeping simple, stress-free, and exciting – all in a uniquely tailored way.


Our Motto: Healthy fish, happy tanks, and unique experiences.
